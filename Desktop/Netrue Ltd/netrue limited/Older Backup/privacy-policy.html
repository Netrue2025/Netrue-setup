Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026.

Netrue Limited ("we", "us", "our") built this website to empower agriculture with technology including IoT, AI, software, and training. This policy explains the types of data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights.

1. Information we collect

  • Personal data submitted through contact forms (name, email, project details, services of interest).
  • Newsletter signups (email address).
  • Device and usage data via cookies for navigation performance and analytics.
  • WhatsApp interaction data when using the WhatsApp chat button/link (via WhatsApp services).
  • Search queries in the AI assistant section (local query text does not leave your browser unless integrated with backend APIs).

2. How we use your information

  • Respond to inquiries, support requests, and quote requests (via Contact Us form or chat).
  • Provide the newsletter and updates about our services (AI engineering, IoT, shop, Academy).
  • Improve website experience, performance, and the Ask Me Anything assistant responses.
  • Deliver relevant marketing content and personalized experiences across our site.

3. Data sharing and third parties

We may share data with service providers needed to operate the website or process communications (hosting, email, analytics tools, WhatsApp). We do not sell your personal information to third-party marketers.

4. Cookie policy

We use cookies to store preferences (theme toggle, responsive changes), collect analytics, and secure sessions. You can control cookie settings through your browser settings.

5. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted transfers (HTTPS). However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your rights

  • Access and review your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.

7. Contact

For data requests or policy questions, reach us using the Contact Us page or email info@netrue.io.
