Our Story

Originally founded as Netrue Limited, we began as a versatile software engineering firm building enterprise solutions for global brands. However, our founders witnessed a critical gap between high-end technology and the agricultural sector—the backbone of our civilization.

Recognizing that food security and sustainability are the most pressing challenges of our time, we pivoted. We rebranded as Netrue Agri-Tech, channeling our deep expertise in AI, IoT, and Software Engineering toward creating a more efficient, tech-enabled farming future.
